✦ About PROVEN PSYCHOLOGY JOURNAL

A Magazine Born from Curiosity

The Proven Psychology Journal is for readers interested in mindfulness and intentional digital usage, as well as deeper aspects of the human psyche.

Questions relating to psychology, mindfulness and intentional practices in the modern world form the core of this journal.

With a research-based and fact-driven approach, we aim to dispel misconceptions and provide our readers with a perspective that helps them to better understand themselves and others.

Furthermore, our mission is to highlight the importance of a healthy mindset and to ensure that topics relating to personality, perception and consciousness are not swept under the carpet. In particular, we seek to create a space of recognition and safety for both individuals and the collective to enrich their awareness in daily life.

Through our articles and podcast, Proven Psychology explores current developments regarding the questions of life, such as mental well-being, personality and self-development.

We take a critical and deeply engaged look at trends in the digital and social worlds, drawing on psychological insights and philosophical principles.
